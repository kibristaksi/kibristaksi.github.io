Ercan Airport Transfer Service

Ercan International Airport (ECN) is the main airport of Northern Cyprus, serving millions of passengers annually. CYP VIP Transfer provides professional private transfer services from Ercan Airport to all destinations across the island.

Start your journey in comfort with our Ercan airport transfer service. Our modern vehicles and experienced drivers ensure a safe, comfortable ride from the airport to your hotel or any address.

Ercan Airport Transfer Routes

  • Ercan → Nicosia: 20-30 minutes
  • Ercan → Kyrenia: 40-50 minutes
  • Ercan → Iskele: 45-60 minutes
  • Ercan → Famagusta: 30-45 minutes
  • Ercan → Bafra: 60-75 minutes
  • Ercan → Karpaz: Comfortable transfer to the Karpaz peninsula
